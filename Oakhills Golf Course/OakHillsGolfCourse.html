About Us

Golf the Way
It Was Meant to Be

Nestled in the rolling landscape of Cerro Gordo County, Oak Hills Golf Course has been a cornerstone of the Clear Lake community for over forty years. Our 9-hole course offers a fair challenge for golfers of every skill level, set against the natural beauty of North Iowa.

From the first bloom of spring to the crisp days of autumn, our properly maintained fairways and greens provide an experience that keeps players coming back season after season.
